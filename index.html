~Banana Pancakes~

Today, I am gonna show you how to make the best banana pancakes. This recipe roughly makes two portions of pancakes.


For ingredients you will need

  • 200g flour
  • 250 ml oat milk
  • 2 mashed bananas
  • 1 tbsp baking soda


How to make the banana pancakes in 5 easy steps

  1. Get all of the ingredients from your fridge or pantry.
  2. Put them in a bowl and mix them together.
  3. Add the dough to a non-stick pan and cook the pancakes.
  4. Add some toppings like apple sauce, mashed berries or maple syrup.
  5. Enjoy!
